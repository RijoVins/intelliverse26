  • ⏱️ Presentation duration: 8–10 minutes per team.
  • ❓ A Q&A session will follow the presentation by the judging panel.
📄 Content Rules
  • 🖼️ Maximum 10 slides allowed in the presentation.

Esports Elite — Free Fire

Squad up and battle it out in the ultimate mobile battle royale. Strategy, teamwork, and aim decide who wins.

📋 General Rules
  • 🚫Gun skins are not allowed.
  • 🎯Points calculated based on kills and final position.
  • 🔌Matches will not be restarted due to individual disconnections.
  • 📊Scoring system will be decided by the coordinators.
🏟️ Match Procedure
  • 🗺️Format: Battle Royale – Squad | Maps: Bermuda, Purgatory, Kalahari
  • 📱Room joining details shared 10–15 minutes before match.
  • ⏱️Teams not present within 5–10 minutes of scheduled time will forfeit.
⚙️ Game Settings
  • ✅Character Skills: Enabled
  • 🚫Gun Attributes: Disabled (Fair Play Settings)
  • 🎮Game Mode: Esports Mode
🔗 Register for Free Fire

E-Football

Face off in a 1v1 e-football battle. Penalty shootouts settle close ties — may the best player win.

📋 Event Rules
  • 👤Mode: 1 vs 1 — solo competition.
  • ⏱️Match Duration: 6 minutes per game.
  • 🥅Knockout tie → resolved by Penalty Shootout.
  • 🔄Maximum substitutions: 5–6 players per match.
  • 📶Players are responsible for their own internet connection & battery.
  • 📸Winners must submit screenshots of match results.
